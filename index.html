The Person Behind the Needle

Meet Sara

Hi there — I'm Sara, the maker and dreamer behind The Curated Clip. What started as a love of embroidery and a habit of hunting through antique shops has turned into something I'm incredibly proud of: a small business built entirely on handcrafted products made with heart.

Every bag charm, keyring, and accessory I make is made by hand, one at a time, in my home studio. I choose high quality materials I love and I put real care into every piece. No two are exactly alike, and that's kind of the point.

I started The Curated Clip because I believe everyday accessories should feel special. Whether you clip one onto a well-worn tote or give it as a gift, I hope each piece brings a little joy into your day.

Come find me at a market near you — I love meeting the people who carry my work out into the world.
